Snake Plant Care Tip

Snake plants are super low-maintenance! I water mine only when the soil is completely dry. They thrive in indirect light and don't need frequent repotting.

Spider Plant Care Tip

Spider plants need a little more attention. I mist mine regularly and keep it in a spot with indirect sunlight. The babies (offshoots) are so cute!
